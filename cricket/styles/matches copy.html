Local Time, 08:41 GMT, 14:11 IST: Disappointing batting performance from Afghanistan. On a good batting wicket they never got going and by the halfway stage had played more than 100 dots as Rahmat and Shahidi failed to rotate the strike enough. Old-school ODI batting of the 80s and early 90s and that allowed the hosts to build the pressure on them. Soon after getting in they got out. Mehidy with a tight spell through the middle overs as he kept a leash on things. Eventually it was left to Najiubullah who played an enterprising knock of 67 to help his side go over 200.
